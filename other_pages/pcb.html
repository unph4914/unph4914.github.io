ECEN 5730 - CU Boulder

What I gained from the Course

I gained proficiency in this course in translating ideas into working prototype circuit boards that are ready for mass production. I acquired the skill of constructing prototypes using multilayer circuit boards and solderless breadboards while implementing industry best practices for risk management, design, and testing. The maintenance of cost and schedule targets was the primary focus of improving troubleshooting, debugging, and manufacturing process understanding skills. My employment in the electronics sector depends on my ability to effectively translate creative ideas into workable solutions, which is made possible by my degree.
